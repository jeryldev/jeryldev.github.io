# Privacy Policy for RingTrack

**Last Updated:** January 25, 2026

## Overview

RingTrack is a privacy-first habit tracking app for iPhone and Apple Watch. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

**We collect ZERO personal data.**

- No email addresses
- No names
- No phone numbers
- No location data
- No device identifiers
- No usage analytics
- No advertising tracking
- No third-party analytics services

## Data Storage

All habit data is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework.

- Your habit names, goals, and progress are stored only on your iPhone and Apple Watch
- Data stays on your devices and is never transmitted to our servers
- We have no servers - there is nothing to transmit data to

## iCloud Sync

RingTrack **does not currently use iCloud sync**.

If we add iCloud sync in a future version:
- Your data would sync between your own Apple devices via your personal iCloud account
- This would be handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure
- We would have no access to your iCloud data
- You could disable iCloud sync in Settings at any time

## Watch-iPhone Sync

Your iPhone and Apple Watch communicate directly with each other using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework:
- Data is transferred locally between your paired devices
- No internet connection is required
- No data passes through our servers

## Tip Jar (In-App Purchases)

RingTrack offers optional tips to support development:
- Tips are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit
- We receive only aggregated sales data from Apple (no personal information)
- We never see your payment information, credit card numbers, or billing address
- Apple handles all payment processing securely

## Notifications

RingTrack uses local notifications to remind you about your habits:
- Notifications are generated on your device
- No push notification servers
- No notification tracking
- You can disable notifications in iOS Settings at any time

## Data You Control

You have complete control over your data:
- **Delete habits:** Swipe to delete any habit and its data
- **Delete the app:** Uninstalling RingTrack permanently deletes all habit data
- **No account:** No account creation means nothing to delete from our servers

## Third-Party Services

RingTrack uses **zero third-party services**:
- No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
- No crash reporting services
- No advertising networks
- No social media integrations

## Children's Privacy

RingTrack does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will:
- Update the "Last Updated" date above
- Notify users through an in-app notification
- Require acceptance of new terms before continuing to use the app

## Data Security

While we don't collect your data, we take security seriously:
- All data is stored using Apple's secure SwiftData framework
- Your device's built-in encryption protects your data at rest
- No network transmission means no interception risk

## Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws, you have rights regarding your data. Since we don't collect your data:
- **Right to access:** All your data is visible in the app
- **Right to deletion:** Delete habits or uninstall the app
- **Right to portability:** Data stays on your device
- **Right to opt-out:** Nothing to opt out of - we don't collect data
